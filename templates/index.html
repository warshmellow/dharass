What is dHarass?

Do you get nasty messages and replies on Twitter that say things like "Die bitch", and other gendered harassment, from random strangers? Sign in with Twitter, and dHarass reads your mentions for this kind of garbage so you don't have too. It brings up the users and the messages for you to see if you want. Block all in one click.

One-click block

Block all terrible users in one click. If they're gonna leave garbage in your mentions, you don't need to hear from them again.

Time saver

Don't waste your time reading gendered harassment garbage, letting it bring you down throughout the week. Let dHarass screen for you.

Machine Learning Powered

dHarass uses advanced machine learning algorithms to flag garbage in your mentions, and learns your preferences the more you use it.

Mobile-friendly

Clean your mentions from your phone.
